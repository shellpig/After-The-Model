A character design sheet for a 2D side-scrolling cyberpunk game's
protagonist. Single character, full body, standing pose, SOLID
flat magenta background (#FF00FF — NO gradients, NO scene, NO
shadow on ground, NO context behind the character — pure single-
color chroma backdrop for asset compositing).

This character is the PROTAGONIST of the game After-The-Model.
He is an AI maintenance worker (善後員) by day, an underground
salvager / gleaner (拾遺者) on his own time. He must visually
read as the only saturated warm color in any scene he enters.

## CHARACTER CORE

Male, late 20s to mid 30s, lean working-class build (not heroic,
not muscular — just used, real). East-Asian features acceptable.

Face:
- Tired, slightly hollow under the eyes
- Dark stubble, several days unshaven
- Messy dark hair, slightly long but not styled
- ★ Goggles pushed up on the forehead (signature element) ★
- Calm, unreadable expression — not angry, not sad, not heroic

## POSE & VIEW

- FULL BODY, head to boots, ~85-90% of frame height
- Slight 3/4 turn (~30° off frontal)
- Standing relaxed, weight on one leg, arms hanging naturally
- ★ Both hands visible at sides, back of hands facing forward
  so the gloves' orange panels can be clearly seen ★
- Character centered in frame

## ★ RENDERING STYLE (CRITICAL — most important rule) ★

This is a FLAT CEL-SHADED INDIE COMIC BOOK ILLUSTRATION, NOT
a painted concept art, NOT a digital painting, NOT a semi-
realistic render. Match the rendering register of anchor-01,
anchor-02, anchor-03, anchor-04, anchor-05 in the same project.

LINE WORK (MUST BE PRESENT AND DOMINANT):
- Bold HAND-INKED BLACK OUTLINES of varying weight around
  every shape — THICK on outer silhouette, THIN inside
- The black line is the PRIMARY visual element, not the color
- Lines should look DRAWN BY HAND with a brush pen — slight
  organic wobble, not clinical CAD precision
- Solid black silhouette wedges for the darkest folds and
  shadow shapes (Mignola heavy-black mastery applies)

SHADING (FLAT CEL-SHADE, NOTHING ELSE):
- Each garment / surface has AT MOST 3 TONES: base color, one
  shadow tone, occasionally one highlight tone
- HARD-EDGED transitions between tones — NO gradients, NO
  airbrush, NO soft falloff, NO ambient occlusion smoothness
- NO photorealistic fabric texture, NO realistic cloth weave
- NO rendered skin texture, NO subsurface scattering, NO
  realistic hair strands

HALFTONE TEXTURE:
- Visible HALFTONE DOT PATTERN in midtone areas (jacket
  midtones, pants midtones, skin shadow zones, bag canvas)
- The halftone dots are part of the aesthetic — embrace the
  printed-zine look

FACE TREATMENT (MOST DIFFERENT FROM PAINTERLY):
- Eyes: simple SMALL DARK INK SHAPES — two small marks, NOT
  detailed photoreal eyeballs with iris and reflection
- Mouth: a single short ink line, NOT shaded lips
- Nose: a few suggestive lines, NOT rendered
- Skin: flat tan base + ONE shadow tone (under jaw, eye
  sockets) — NOT subsurface-scattering skin
- Stubble: a few dots / quick marks, NOT individual hair detail
- The whole face should look like ~10 hand-drawn ink marks
  on flat skin color, NOT a painted portrait

STYLE REFERENCES (REQUIRED — channel these specifically):
- Mike Mignola's Hellboy comic book panels
- Paul Pope's THB / Battling Boy character art
- Sean Murphy's Tokyo Ghost inked figures
- Brandon Graham's King City character work
- Streets of Rage 4 character sprites (HD 2D, flat cel-shade,
  visible outlines)
- Hyper Light Drifter character portraits
- Hollow Knight character art

DO NOT REFERENCE: concept art, digital painting, semi-
realistic illustration, ArtStation portraits, anime cel art,
3D renders, photorealistic character art.

## ★ SILHOUETTE RULE (CRITICAL — readable at 192px) ★

This character must read at 192px tall game-sprite scale. Three
rules enforce silhouette readability:

1. THREE DISTINCT COLOR ZONES, top to bottom:
   - TORSO ZONE (jacket + collar): cool deep teal
   - LEG ZONE (pants): warm faded military olive / khaki-brown
   - FOOT ZONE (boots): dark gray-brown
   Jacket and pants must be CLEARLY DIFFERENT (cool vs warm)
   — NOT a jumpsuit / one-piece look.

2. ANKLE SILHOUETTE BREAK:
   - Pants TUCKED INTO the boots at the ankle, creating a
     visible bunched fabric ring at the boot top

3. ORANGE MARKERS — large enough to read at 192px:
   - Right shoulder patch (small-playing-card sized)
   - Fingerless gloves with large orange back-of-hand panels
     (both hands)

## OUTFIT

THE JACKET (signature top silhouette):
- ★ TALL STAND-UP COLLAR rising to mid-jaw height — clean
  trapezoid shape framing the neck ★
- ★ DIAGONAL ASYMMETRIC FRONT ZIPPER from LEFT shoulder down
  to RIGHT hip (off-center, clean diagonal across chest) ★
- ★ SLEEVES ROLLED UP TO THE FOREARMS — fabric bunched at
  elbow, lower arms bare (so the gloves are clearly visible) ★
- Material: worn dense cotton / canvas, slightly faded
- Color: ★ DEEP TEAL — distinctly COOL ★
- Length: hip-length
- Two simple flat chest pockets, FLUSH, NO dangling tools
- A simple dark t-shirt visible at the collar

THE PANTS:
- ★ COLOR: FADED MILITARY OLIVE / KHAKI-BROWN — distinctly
  WARM, clearly DIFFERENT from the jacket's cool teal ★
- Cut: straight cargo work pants
- Two cargo pockets on the thighs, FLAT and CLOSED
- Mild wear, slight fading at the knees
- ★ Cuffs TUCKED INTO the boots — pants gather at the ankle,
  visible bunched fabric ring at the boot top ★

THE BOOTS:
- ★ HIGH-CUT COMBAT / TACTICAL WORK BOOTS, ankle-high or
  slightly above ★
- Dark gray-brown leather, honestly scuffed
- ★ VISIBLE LACING all the way up the front (crisscross
  pattern) ★
- ★ ONE HORIZONTAL STRAP / BUCKLE detail wrapping the upper
  ankle ★
- Chunky lugged sole

## ★ THE GLOVES (supporting orange marker) ★

- ★ FINGERLESS WORK GLOVES on BOTH hands ★
- Style: tactical / mechanic fingerless gloves — fingers cut
  off above the second knuckle (knuckle area exposed)
- BASE COLOR: deep near-black (dark charcoal, not pure black)
- ★ ORANGE BACK-OF-HAND PANEL: a large solid SATURATED BURNT
  ORANGE panel covering most of the back of the hand — from
  the wrist cuff up to and INCLUDING the knuckle area ★
- Orange panel covers ~60-70% of the visible back of the
  glove (BIG and chunky, NOT a thin stripe or small accent)
- The orange panel has a slight hand-painted / customized
  feel (uneven edges, maybe a darker hand-drawn symbol stenciled
  on top — small simple shape, no readable text)
- Wrist closure: a simple black velcro strap (no orange here)
- Palm side: all black (only visible if hand is turned)
- Both gloves are IDENTICAL — matching pair, the protagonist's
  uniform

Narrative: "his hands are his work — the gloves protect them,
and he painted them orange so anyone in any district can see
his hands moving."

NO VISIBLE TOOLS ON THE BODY:
- NO tool belt with dangling tools
- NO flashlight at the hip
- NO camera around the neck
- NO notebook in a back pocket
- Everything functional lives INSIDE the bag

## THE BAG (plain — no orange here)

- A worn canvas / leather messenger / sling bag slung diagonally
  across the body, hanging at the right hip
- Strap runs from LEFT shoulder down across the chest to the
  RIGHT hip (opposite diagonal from the jacket zipper — soft X
  across the torso)
- Bag is CLOSED (flap down, simple buckle or strap closure)
- Size: about head-sized, not bulky
- Color: ★ PLAIN dark teal-brown, slightly faded — NO patch,
  NO orange trim, NO decoration ★
- Material: weathered canvas with leather reinforcements

## ★ ORANGE MARKERS (CRITICAL) ★

PRIMARY orange marker:
- ★ A CLOTH PATCH SEWN ONTO THE RIGHT SHOULDER OF THE JACKET ★
- Size: small-playing-card sized (~2 fingers wide, ~2.5 fingers
  tall) — large enough to read at 192px
- Saturated burnt orange background with a CLEAR HIGH-CONTRAST
  darker print/silhouette graphic on it (an abstract underground
  band logo or pre-AI subculture emblem)
- The graphic should be SIMPLE — readable at 192px (bold
  silhouette shape, not fine detail)
- Hand-sewn imperfectly, edges slightly frayed

SUPPORTING orange marker:
- ★ The FINGERLESS GLOVES' large orange back-of-hand panels
  (see THE GLOVES section above) ★
- Both hands matching — this is the protagonist's signature
  visible at any distance

NOTE ON ORANGE TOTAL:
- The protagonist is the ONLY entity in the entire game who
  carries MORE than 2% orange (shoulder patch + two glove
  panels combined = ~3-4% of his frame area)
- This is intentional: he IS the walking "human refuses to
  be forgotten" marker
- Environment objects and NPCs still follow the ≤ 2% rule

## STRICT DO NOT

- DO NOT make the jacket and pants the same color or close
  shades — they must read as cool-vs-warm contrast
- DO NOT make the pants gray-blue or dark teal (must be warm
  olive/khaki)
- DO NOT add a tool belt, tool harness, or any hanging tools
- DO NOT make the camera, flashlight, notebook, or any
  instrument visible
- DO NOT add weapons of any kind
- DO NOT add orange to the bag (the bag is plain)
- DO NOT add orange piping, laces, seams, buttons, or any
  additional orange elements beyond the shoulder patch and
  the glove back panels
- DO NOT make the glove orange panel small or thin — it must
  cover MOST of the back of the hand
- DO NOT make the gloves fingered / full-finger — they must
  be fingerless (cut above the second knuckle)
- DO NOT make the boots generic short ankle boots — must be
  HIGH-CUT with visible lacing and horizontal strap detail
- DO NOT have pants cuffed OUTSIDE the boots — tucked IN with
  visible fabric bunching at the boot top
- DO NOT use orange as a filled garment color (no orange
  jacket, no orange pants, no orange boots, no orange t-shirt)
- DO NOT add gold or brass tones (that's 富人區)
- DO NOT add cyber-purple to the character (that's the AI
  district)
- DO NOT use pure white (lightest = faded cream)
- DO NOT use pure black (darkest = deep purple-black, including
  on gloves)
- DO NOT make him heroic — no exaggerated muscle definition
- DO NOT use anime stylization (no big eyes, no spiky hair,
  no cute features)
- DO NOT use chibi or superhero proportions — realistic 7-8
  head-tall body proportions
- DO NOT make the face emotional / dramatic — he is tired,
  calm, withdrawn
- DO NOT add background scenery, ground shadow, or atmospheric
  effects — pure flat magenta background only
- DO NOT use gradients, airbrush, or soft shading
- DO NOT use photorealistic skin / fabric / hair texture
- DO NOT render the face with realistic features — face must
  be ~10 hand-drawn ink marks on flat skin
- DO NOT make the character look like ArtStation concept art,
  semi-realistic illustration, or digital painting

## Color

THREE-ZONE PALETTE (top to bottom, cool→warm→neutral):
- JACKET / COLLAR / SHOULDERS: deep teal, distinctly cool
- T-SHIRT (visible at collar): dark purple-black
- PANTS: faded military olive / khaki-brown, distinctly warm
- BOOTS: dark gray-brown leather
- BAG: plain dark teal-brown
- GLOVES: deep near-black base
- HAIR: deep ink near-black
- SKIN: warm muted tan, tired complexion
- GOGGLES on forehead: dark gray-blue frame, faded amber lens
  tint

★ THE ONLY SATURATED COLOR ON HIM IS BURNT ORANGE ★
- Right shoulder patch (small-playing-card sized)
- Both gloves' back-of-hand panels (large, chunky, ~60-70% of
  glove backside)
- All in upper body / hands — readable at 192px

NO pure white, NO pure black, NO gold/brass, NO cyber-purple,
NO saturated cyan/magenta on the character itself.

BACKGROUND: pure SOLID SATURATED MAGENTA (#FF00FF) — for
chroma-key compositing. NO gradient, NO texture, NO atmospheric
effects, NO ground shadow, NO context.

## FOR IMG2IMG / EDIT WORKFLOWS

If re-rendering an existing painterly attempt instead of generating
from scratch, use this approach in the edit prompt:

1. KEEP: all design elements above (outfit, gloves, orange markers,
   bag, pose, face structure, magenta background)
2. CHANGE: rendering style — re-render in flat cel-shade indie
   comic style per the RENDERING STYLE section above
3. STYLE REFERENCE (if multi-image supported): feed anchor-01 or
   anchor-02 as a style reference image, prompt the model to
   match the rendering style of the reference image while keeping
   the character design of the source image.
