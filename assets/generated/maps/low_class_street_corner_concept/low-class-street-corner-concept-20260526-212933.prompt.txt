Near-future 2030 lower-class cyberpunk city street corner cross-section, 2D side-scrolling game environment concept art.

Scene:
Nighttime street corner in the lower level of the city. In the center is a 24-hour unmanned convenience store. The entrance has glowing neon signage made of abstract unreadable shapes, a vending machine, and trash bins packed with discarded objects. On the right side is a narrow alley with a metal fire escape climbing toward the rooftops. On the left side, the street extends outward into the distance, with more low-rise buildings and a few airborne advertising projections. The ground is wet old concrete with puddles, oil stains, cigarette butts, stepped-on paper scraps, and street graffiti.

Core style: American cyberpunk comic art plus traces-of-memory aesthetics.

The whole image should feel like an object that has been used for a long time:
- crooked, faded, peeling storefront signs
- walls covered in layered old posters, stickers, spray paint, and graffiti, newer marks covering older marks
- scratched metal surfaces, rust, welded repair marks
- vending machine with stickers, dents from being kicked, hand-patched price labels without readable text
- every object should carry traces that people were once here

Comic technique:
- American independent cyberpunk comic style
- influenced by Heavy Metal magazine, Paul Pope, Brandon Graham's King City, Geof Darrow, and Sean Murphy
- hand-drawn ink linework with varied line weight, not uniform digital 1-pixel outlines
- large deep shadow shapes for silhouette and contrast
- halftone / screentone texture in midtone shadow areas
- flat cel-shading, no smooth gradient transitions
- occasional 1-2 pixel risograph color misregistration

Color:
- overall muted, deep, low-saturation palette
- base colors: deep purple-black, deep teal, muddy plum, dusty olive
- small accent colors: burnt orange, faded mustard, cold cyan, oxblood
- neon magenta and cyan only on light sources such as signs, ads, vending machine glow, and vehicle lights
- no pure white; brightest value should be faded warm cream
- no pure black; darkest value should be deep purple-black

Composition:
- pure 2D side view, eye-level player viewpoint standing at the street corner
- wide horizontal side-scroller composition
- readable parallax depth: foreground trash bins and hanging signs, midground convenience store and alley entrance, background building silhouettes and airborne ads
- depth should come from layered silhouettes and overlap, not strong perspective
- no characters, no vehicles as the main subject, no UI

Forbidden:
- no readable text, no UI, no logos, no frame, no signature, no watermark
- no overly clean smooth surfaces
- no Japanese anime lighting, no photorealism, no 3D-rendered look
