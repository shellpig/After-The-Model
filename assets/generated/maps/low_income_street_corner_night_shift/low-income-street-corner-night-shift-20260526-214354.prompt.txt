A 2030 low-income cyberpunk city street corner, horizontal cross-section
view for a 2D side-scrolling game scene concept.

## Scene
Late night, just after rain. At center, a 24-hour unmanned convenience
store with warm white interior light spilling through the glass onto
the wet ground. A flickering neon sign and a vending machine outside.
On the right, a narrow alley with a metal fire escape stretching to
rooftops; cardboard boxes piled near the alley mouth. On the left, the
street extends outward with more low-rise buildings, aerial advertisement
projections, and a thin rain mist in the distance. The ground is wet,
puddles reflecting neon. The street is almost empty — only a blurred
silhouette of one passerby in the far distance.

## Core Style: American Cyberpunk Comic × "Tired Night Shift in a City That No Longer Needs Humans"

The whole image captures a single moment of a city where humans are
no longer essential, still running tonight but eerily quiet:
- Strong light-dark contrast; the convenience store light is the
  emotional center of the frame
- Large fields of shadow and deep color; light concentrated on windows,
  signs, and water reflections
- Wet atmosphere; neon light stretching long on the ground
- One or two signs flickering / showing CRT ghosting
- Lonely, cold, but with a faint warmth (convenience store amber glow)

## Comic Technique
- American indie cyberpunk comic aesthetic
- Influenced by: Frank Miller's Sin City (light-dark contrast),
  Sean Murphy's Tokyo Ghost, Paul Pope, Becky Cloonan
- Hand-inked line work with varying line weight
- **Strong chiaroscuro**: large black ink shapes versus strong light
  sources (high contrast is the primary visual signature)
- Halftone dots used in midtones — especially ground, walls, rain mist
- Flat cel-shade; no smooth gradient transitions
- Occasional risograph misregistration for print-era feel

## Color
- Overall deep, cold, near-monochrome feel; only small accent splashes
- Base: deep teal, ink purple-black, cold cyan, grayish blue
- Warm accents (only on light sources and inside the store):
  burnt orange, faded mustard, warm white interior light
- Neon (magenta, cyan) on signs, puddle reflections, aerial ads
- No pure white (lightest = warm cream / faded cream, concentrated
  inside the store)
- No pure black (darkest = deep purple-black)

## Composition
- Strict 2D side view, eye-level on the street
- Multi-layer parallax: foreground (puddles, debris, sign reflections) /
  midground (convenience store, alley entrance) /
  background (rain mist, building silhouettes, aerial ads)
- Compositional emphasis on "central light source + surrounding darkness"
  — stage-lighting effect

## Do NOT
No text, UI, logo, frame, signature, watermark.
No daytime scene. No dry scene (must have wetness / post-rain / reflections).
No anime-style lighting.
No photorealistic look.
No 3D-rendered appearance.
