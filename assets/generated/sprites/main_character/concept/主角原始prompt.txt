A character design sheet for a 2D side-scrolling cyberpunk game'sprotagonist. Single character, full body, standing pose, SOLIDflat magenta background (#FF00FF — NO gradients, NO scene, NOshadow on ground, NO context behind the character — pure single-color chroma backdrop for asset compositing).

CHARACTER CORE

Male, late 20s to mid 30s, lean working-class build (not heroic,not muscular — just used, real). East-Asian features acceptable.

Face:

Tired, slightly hollow under the eyes

Dark stubble, several days unshaven

Messy dark hair, slightly long but not styled

★ Goggles pushed up on the forehead (signature element) ★

Calm, unreadable expression — not angry, not sad, not heroic

POSE & VIEW

FULL BODY, head to boots, ~85-90% of frame height

Slight 3/4 turn (~30° off frontal)

Standing relaxed, weight on one leg, arms hanging naturally

★ Both hands visible at sides, back of hands facing forwardso the gloves' orange panels can be clearly seen ★

Character centered in frame

★ SILHOUETTE RULE (CRITICAL — read before painting) ★

This character must read at 192px tall game-sprite scale. ThreeRULES enforce silhouette readability:

THREE DISTINCT COLOR ZONES, top to bottom:

TORSO ZONE (jacket + collar): cool deep teal

LEG ZONE (pants): warm faded military olive / khaki-brown

FOOT ZONE (boots): dark gray-brownThe jacket and pants must be CLEARLY DIFFERENT (cool vs warm)— NOT a jumpsuit / one-piece look.

ANKLE SILHOUETTE BREAK:

Pants TUCKED INTO the boots at the ankle, creating avisible bunched fabric ring at the boot top

ORANGE MARKERS — large enough to read at 192px:

Right shoulder patch (small-playing-card sized)

Fingerless gloves with large orange back-of-hand panels(both hands)

Mike Mignola heavy-black mastery — strong black silhouetteedge on the body, the boots, the bag, the hair, the gloves
